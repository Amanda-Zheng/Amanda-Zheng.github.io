About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI) Discipline, School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University, Australia. Before this, I was an Assistant Professor in the School of ICT at Griffith University, Australia, and a member of TrustAGI Lab led by Prof. Shirui Pan. My research focuses on Data-centric AI, Automated Graph Machine Learning, and AI for Science. My work has been featured in prestigious journals and conferences, such as IJCV, ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, and WWW, etc. I was also a recipient of the 2024 Postgraduate Publications Award from Monash University.
