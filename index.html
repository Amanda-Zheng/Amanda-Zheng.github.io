About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI) Discipline, School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University, Australia. Before this, I was an Assistant Professor in the School of ICT at Griffith University and a member of TrustAGI Lab led by Prof. Shirui Pan. My research focuses on Data-centric AI, Automated Graph Machine Learning, and AI for Science.
